Indoor Sleeping

Vastly popular among people from ancient times. Suggested consumation through eight hours during every night at a moderately temperated comfortable place for long term good health.

Outdoor Sleeping

Gaining enormous popularity among most age groups during the current and previous century. No consistent long-term health implications.

Group Sleeping

Sleeping with peers and loved ones in a shared space can be beneficial for the body, mind and social fiber. Long term health implications favorable for most people.
